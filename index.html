About

Dungeons and Dragons (D&D or DnD) is a highly customizable and adaptable tabletop role-playing game that empowers players to create unique and immersive fantasy worlds. With a flexible rule system, players can personalize their characters, craft intricate narratives, and modify the game to suit their preferences. The game's expansiveness allows for limitless creativity, making it a dynamic and ever-evolving experience where imagination takes center stage.

MMORPDND (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Dungeons and Dragons) strives to serve as an open-source Dungeons and Dragons universe, welcoming contributions from all enthusiasts. The objective is to craft a solar system that boasts coherence, depth, and meticulous detail, providing a sturdy framework for individuals to seamlessly infuse their personal creativity into DND campaigns. Central to this endeavor are high-definition maps and landscapes, available for players to lay claim upon, adapting and tailoring them to suit their unique objectives. The architecture is designed with simplicity in mind, incorporating specialized tools to ensure organization and user-friendly navigation for effortless expansions.

Presently, the project is in its infancy. The initial launch will encompass the debut of the first fully developed planet, primed for utilization by fellow creators.

How to use MMORPDND

For Players and Enthusiasts:

  1. Accessing the Database: Visit this website at MMORPDND.github.io. Use the "Database" button in the navigation bar.
  2. Exploring the Content: Click "Database" to access the D&D information.
  3. Viewing Details: Click on an entry and explore the subdirectories for detailed information about creatures, spells, items, and more.

For Developers:

  1. Front End Development: The MMORPDND.github.io site is the main front-end repository at MMORPDND Github.
  2. Back End/Database Development: The bulk of the database information and main DnD repository is located in a separate repository at DnD GitHub. This repo is used as a submodule within this main MMORPDND.github.io repo which simply serves as a front end for sharing and users.
  3. Developer Docs: The development docs have all the information needed for contributing and developers. See the manual located here - Developer Manual.

For All Users:

  1. Stay Informed: Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter.
  2. Providing Feedback: Contact us with feedback, issues, or contributions.
  3. Contributing: Learn how to contribute to our open-source project.

Thank you for choosing MMORPDND as your ultimate D&D resource. Whether you're exploring our database or contributing as a developer, we're here to enhance your tabletop adventures!
