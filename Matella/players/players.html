Lee Bruce (Monk) - Sean

At the age of 8, Lee's parents gave him to the monastery of "Verum Sui" where he would spend the rest of his life in study and training. Lee's family had lived in the village (Shou-Lang) outside of the monastery for generations, it was a tradition to give one's firstborn to the monastery and Lee's family had been a part of this tradition for as long as his family had lived there.

In the beginning, Lee was a part of a group of children who were all beginning their training and were given the title of Novice. Lee spent the next 7 years in training as a novice where he found his love for reading and writing. As a changeling, Lee would often try to take the forms of the people he would read about and admired most, (as well as characters he created himself) his teachers did not always approve of this and would frequently correct him when they would see him doing it. One day Father Zalfay saw Lee doing this and pulled him aside and told him, "Always be yourself, express yourself, have faith in yourself, do not seek out a successful personality and try to duplicate it. These great men that you admire, then never wanted to be just like one of their heros, they wanted to become the best that they could and in turn became not a copy of someone else, but a new powerful person of their own." From this moment on, Lee no longer took the form of others that he had admired, but would come to create his own form that he would then live by.

At the end of Lee's 7 years as a novice, he was requested by Father Zalfay and began his training as a Rassaphore under him. Zalfay and Lee became close as Lee's training and study progressed, a bond that began more as a father and son, soon became that of brother to brother, though Zalfay was still Lee's Stavrophore.

As the year of the scribe came near, it was becoming clear that Lee was to be the next scribe. Zalfay was concerned for Lee due to Lee's tendency to change into a new personality from time to time, but Zalfay believed in Lee and did not object when the council told Lee that he was to be the next Scribe. Though Lee would not yet be a Stavrophore when he began his journey as the Scribe, he knew that during this journey he would be able to come before the council and be tested to become a Stavrophore. Lee was honored to become the Scribe as there was only one Scribe every 60 years.

With the parting words from Zalfay "remember brother, it will be hard or out there but do not pray for an easy life, pray for the strength to endure a difficult one. This journey is on your shoulders, but you are always welcome to return when you are in need of rest or assistance. Remember your ki"

Lee left the monastery and began his journey as a Scribe.

Upcoming Level up abilities

This is a section where I can store things the players will receive when leveling up that way I can incorporate how their characters acquire new skills and traits into the story.

Parker (Rogue) - Allie

Gains at level two

  • Starting at 2nd level, your quick thinking and agility allow you to move and act quickly. You can take a bonus action on each of your turns in combat. This action can be used only to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.

Zinnia Zenchua (Druid) - Kira

Gains at level two

  • Starting at 2nd level, you can use your action to magically assume the shape of a beast that you have seen before. You can use this feature twice. You regain expended uses when you finish a short or long rest.
  • At 2nd level, you choose to identify with a circle of druids: the Circle of the Land detailed at the end of the class description or one from the Player's Handbook or other sources.

Cedric Mhurren (Barbarian) - Seamus

Gains at level two

  • Starting at 2nd level, you can throw aside all concern for defense to attack with fierce desperation. When you make your first attack on your turn, you can decide to attack recklessly. Doing so gives you advantage on melee weapon attack rolls using Strength during this turn, but attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.
  • At 2nd level, you gain an uncanny sense of when things nearby aren’t as they should be, giving you an edge when you dodge away from danger. You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects that you can see, such as traps and spells. To gain this benefit, you can’t be blinded, deafened, or incapacitated.

Lee Bruce (Monk) - Sean

Gains at level two

  • Starting at 2nd level, your training allows you to harness the mystic energy of ki. Your access to this energy is represented by a number of ki points. Your monk level determines the number of points you have, as shown in the Ki Points column of the Monk table.
  • Starting at 2nd level, your speed increases by 10 feet while you are not wearing armor or wielding a shield. This bonus increases when you reach certain monk levels, as shown in the Monk table.
