The Fang Of Elaine

This legendary blade is one of a set (the other's being The Fang Of Pellinore, The Fang Of Azshira, and The Fang Of Essieldore). It appears as though to be forged from a hardened crystal that emits a faint light blue mist. The crystal is slightly transparent.

This blade was forged alongside its paired blade and contains the soul of Elaine Loredmeyer and essence of her abilities. As the user wields this dagger, the soul inside will become more and more attuned to it's beholder. Depending on the alignment of the wielder, this can provide various capabilities to the user.

Stats

This weapon is considered a dagger and does 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 frost damage. After becoming increasingly attuned to the weapon and the soul it contains, the soul within this weapon begins to allow its influence to help you in battle and gains the following effects.

  • Level 3: Gains +2 to all attack rolls.
  • Level 6: Gains the Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 9: Gains the Soul Frost Bite (See below)
  • Level 12: Gains the Improved Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 15: Gains the Improved Soul Frost Bite (See below)
  • Level 18: This weapon gains 1d8 frost damage on each hit.

Good Alignment

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you can choose to siphon 1d4 frost damage from the enemy and heal yourself.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Soul Frost Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you can choose to inflict a Frosty chill to the soul of the target. This lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will take 1d4 extra frost damage once per turn on a melee attack against it. If this effect is already on the target, you can choose to further chill the targets soul on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets movement by half next round.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Neutral Alignment

You must choose either the above or below effects, this choice cannot be changed.

Evil Alignment

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you deal 1d8 frost damage to the enemy.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d8 (Increasing to a total of 2d8).

Soul Frost Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you inflict a Frosty chill to the soul of the target. This lasts for one hour or until dispelled. The target will take 1d4 extra frost damage once per turn on a melee attack against it. If this effect is already on the target, you further chill the targets soul on a subsequent melee attack, which will decrease the targets movement by half next round.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).
