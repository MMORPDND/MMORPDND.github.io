The Fang Of Pellinore

This legendary blade is one of a set (the other's being The Fang Of Elaine, The Fang Of Azshira, and The Fang Of Essieldore). It appears as though to be forged from a hardened crystal that emits a faint light blue mist. The crystal is slightly transparent.

This blade was forged alongside its paired blade and contains the soul of Pellinore Loredmeyer and essence of his abilities. As the user wields this dagger, the soul inside will become more and more attuned to it's beholder. Depending on the alignment of the wielder, this can provide various capabilities to the user.

Stats

This weapon is considered a dagger and does 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 psychic damage. After becoming increasingly attuned to the weapon and the soul it contains, the soul within this weapon begins to allow its influence to help you in battle and gains the following effects.

  • Level 3: Gains +2 to all attack rolls.
  • Level 6: Gains the Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 9: Gains the Soul Psychic Bite (See below)
  • Level 12: Gains the Improved Tapped Soul Effect (See below)
  • Level 15: Gains the Improved Soul Psychic Bite (See below)
  • Level 18: This weapon gains 1d8 Psychic damage on each hit.

Good Alignment

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you can choose to siphon 1d4 psychic damage from the enemy and heal yourself.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d4 (Increasing to a total of 2d4).

Soul psychic Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you can choose to curse the mind of the target with psychic phantasms. The target must succeed a wisdom saving throw or have 2d4 psychic damage siphoned from them to the attacker (they take 2d4, and the attacker gains the damage dealt as health). The DC for this is equal to 10 + your wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 2d4 (Increasing to a total of 4d4).

Neutral Alignment

You must choose either the above or below effects, this choice cannot be changed.

Evil Alignment

Tapped Soul (Effect)

On each strike of the weapon, you deal 1d8 psychic damage to the enemy.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 1d8 (Increasing to a total of 2d8).

Soul psychic Bite

When you make a melee attack with this weapon you curse the mind of the target with psychic phantasms. The target must succeed a wisdom saving throw or take 2d8 psychic damage. The DC for this is equal to 10 + your wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus.

Improved Effect: The Tapped soul effect gains an extra 2d8 (Increasing to a total of 4d8).
