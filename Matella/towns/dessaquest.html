Dessaquest

Dessaquest is a small town founded by Bhomian Dessa many generations ago. It has become a small humble town that has attracted people who do not much enjoy the big city life but would rather stick to a specialized trade while keeping to themselves. Despite this, the town is very close knit and everyone has a high respect for each other.

The Shipyard

The shipyard of Dessaquest is the main entrance that most tracers arrive at. Although there are paths to the northeast and nothest side, these lead to long trails that only traders who don't have a ship or explorers usually travel. The dockmasters' names are Elsharin, day watch) and Toshiro (night watch). As they have plenty of time, these two also repair and create ships and ship accessories that they can offer for sale.

The Tavern: Bubbly Breeze

As most towns have, Dessaquest has a lovely Tavern. Many wines and ales imported from the surrounding region are offered here. The tavern's name is a play on words and somewhat named after it's owner Bre Gilhart. It is run by her family which consists of herself, her husband Lincoln, and her two children Serene and Kendall. They are all human. The tavern does not double as a place ot stay in town here, there is a separate place for that. However, the upstairs to the tavern does indeed have bedrooms but they are used for hte family that run the tavern.

Loren's Lumber Mill

Maxwell Loren owns a Lumber mill that is worked by him and his two sons, Jean and Tenoth. The Mill focuses on creating lumber for building and a special kind of sap chips used for animal feed.

Kendril's Smithery

Kendril's Smithery is ran by Kendril Megatto. Kendril has an apprentice, Dendra Tult, who has been working as a smither for a few years. Kendril specializes in precision tools and building materials, which Dendra has taken a liking to more weapon forging, which he is still a novice at.

StarBrooke Graveyard

The graveyard is just a typical graveyard.

Dessala Farms

The Dessala Farms are primarily run by the descendants of Bhomian Dessa. This includes Tonya Dessala, Bren Dessala (Married to Tonya), and Gilb Dessalla. They typically hire other young hands from the village when further assistance is needed. The specialized crops grown here include an Einkorn Wheat and and blue corn called Maeza corn. Maeza is a primary ingredient in many clear alcohols created throughout the region. The farms are home to a variety of animals such as sheep, horses, hogs, and a large breed of bullfrog.

Denko's Library

Berinold Denko runs the library and spends his time reading and collecting knowledge for the Dessaquest library.

Flourishing Floristry

Maybell Maclure is a young (mid 40's) lady who focuses entirely on growing and crossbreading flowers and plants. She spreads and plants her flowers around town to give it a much more inviting.
