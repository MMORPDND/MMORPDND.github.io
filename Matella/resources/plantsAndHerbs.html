Plants and Herbs

  • Bogbean plant - A main ingredient in 'kush', a mind expanding psychedelic.
  • Chives
  • Comfry - In area's where this grows, it grows like a weed. The plant is large with big fussy and soft leaves. This soft leaf is commonly used to help speed up open cuts and wound recovery. It helps prevent scarring and faster healing.
  • Lillysuckle - This plant has large fern like leaves that are a dark green. In its center there is a large red lilly. The flowers are used in many gourmet dishes and the leaves provide great properties for wound healing and stitching.
  • Stranglekelp - This herb tends to grow under water and is typically a longer stalk with small green leaves growing from it. It is known for adapting to various dangers of sea creatures and is often used to cure poisons from sea creatures.

Flowers

  • Peacebloom - this white flower is often used as a base for other potions and elixers. It is known to improve the potency and combination of various ingredients and has a very large water content which is evident when being crushed.
  • Mageroyal - This plant is edible and used in various recipes. It is a red glistening flower that is known to grow in fertile areas where recent magical activities have occurred. It is often used in potions related to wisdom.

Roots and Barks

  • Briarthorn - This is a 'parasitic' root that tends to grow agaisnt other trees and feed off their nutrients. It tends to wrap itself around the roots of other things. This root is edible and goof for swiftness potions, as it contains high levels of potassium and magnesium.
  • Deathroot - Outwardly identical to Liferoot, this herb has significantly thicker strands when broken open. The thick strands allow for retention and creation of various toxins that is used to ward off predators and allows the Liferoot to grow. This herb can cause severe allergic reactions and is known to act as poison to those who consume it.
  • Earthroot - Can be found along cliff faces and hills and tends to grow on the sides of cliffs facing away from the sunrise. This root is high in phytonutrients and is known for it's ability to provide high energy when consumed. It's often used in vitality and fortitude potions.
  • Liferoot - A green and brown herb. When split open, the inside contains many thin strands, almost like tiny muscle fibers. This herb has many medicinal properties and is often used to treat hangovers.

Berries and Fruits

  • Dayberry - This berry is a bright yellow. It is sweet with a bitter aftertaste.
